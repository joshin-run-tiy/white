White Vision

Sakura

Japan, the place where cherry blossoms bloom in spring. 'Kirei ne.' The envy of all the world. Each blossom, once a bud, now a wonder. We bloom too. 'Bloom where you are planted.'

Master

“It matters not how strait the gate,
How charged with punishments the scroll,
I am the master of my fate:
I am the captain of my soul.”
― William Ernest Henley

In life, the true master is one who master's self.

Heard

Here, your stage. A mic that can fill the world. What would you say? What's your message? Let your voice be heard.

From the tops of the mountains to the depths of the valley's, let your voice be heard. To the ears of the lost...into the hearts of human kind. Let your voice be heard.

Rise

The heights by great men reached and kept
were not attained by sudden flight,
but they, while their companions slept,
were toiling upward in the night.
- Henry Wadsworth Longfellow

Sam

Meet Sam. A glimpse into childhood past. There, in his eyes is the future. Not a care in the world which is how it should be at his age. The story of a boy is told in the face, where a smile is truly worth a thousand other smiles. And that hat, fitting for the chap. Remember, all of us were his age once. Sometimes, we should have a 'not a care in the world' moment too, to get away from all that adultness.

Equus Quagga

A line from a song with the children's tune, ABCs.

"Z is almost always for 'zebra', it is true.
No two are alike, and there's only one of you."

We have something to offer that others do not. True value.

Still

When once the sound of it was as clear as a bell, or the sound when the hammer action function initiated by the given piano key when pressed, on the upper most string furthest from middle 'C'. That inner song, both new and old. Perhaps it is only a memory, a thought, a dream. But it's there, still. It always was...

Motion

One great man once said, "If you can't fly then run, if you can't run then walk, if you can't walk then crawl, but whatever you do you have to keep moving forward." (Martin Luther King Jr.)

In every way, no matter what, never stop your motion forward, forward, forward...

Illuminate

Illuminate the way for another, and cast off the shadow of doubt. Light up the minds of others. The shores of the earth have hungered after the sailor of the seas. But the faithful lighthouse has kept it's light burning along the shore. Shine on.
